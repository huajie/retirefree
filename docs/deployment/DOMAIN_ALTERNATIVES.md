## Domain Search Strategy

### Step 1: Bulk Check Availability
Use domain registrars to check all Tier 1 options:
- **Namecheap**: namecheap.com/domains/registration
- **Cloudflare Registrar**: cloudflare.com (best pricing, ~$9/year)
- **Google Domains**: domains.google.com

### Step 2: Prioritization Criteria
- ✅ Available as .com (preferred)
- ✅ Under $15/year registration cost
- ✅ No trademark conflicts
- ✅ Easy to spell over phone/in person
- ✅ Passes the "radio test" (say it once, people remember it)

### Step 3: Trademark Search
Before finalizing, check:
- **USPTO Trademark Search**: uspto.gov/trademarks
- **Google search**: "{domain name} retirement" to see competitors

## Backup Plan: Alternative TLDs

If .com is unavailable for ALL options:

### Acceptable Alternatives (in order of preference):
1. **.ai** - Perfect for AI-powered products (e.g., retirewise.ai, retireai.ai)
2. **.co** - Professional, widely accepted (e.g., retirewise.co)
3. **.io** - Tech-friendly, startup vibe (e.g., retirewise.io)
4. **.app** - Modern, for web apps (e.g., retirewise.app)

### Avoid:
- ❌ .net (outdated)
- ❌ .org (non-profit association)
- ❌ .biz (spammy reputation)

## SEO Considerations

### Keyword-Rich Domains (Good for SEO):
- retirecalc.com
- retirementcalculator.ai
- airetirementplan.com

### Brandable Domains (Better for long-term):
- retireai.com
- retiro.com
- drawwise.com

**Strategy**: Keyword-rich domain helps with initial organic traffic, but brandable domain is better for viral growth and word-of-mouth.

**Founder Decision**: Choose based on go-to-market strategy:
- **Organic SEO focus** → Pick keyword-rich (retirecalc.com, retirepath.com)
- **Paid ads + viral** → Pick brandable (retireai.com, retirewell.com)
