### 2. Sitemap.xml (Search Engine Discovery)
- **File:** `app/sitemap.ts`
- **URL:** https://retirefree.app/sitemap.xml (auto-generated by Next.js)
- **Pages included:**
  - / (homepage) - Priority 1.0
  - /pricing - Priority 0.8
  - /privacy - Priority 0.5
  - /terms - Priority 0.5

**What this does:**
- Tells Google which pages to index
- Specifies update frequency
- Sets crawl priority
- Speeds up indexing of new pages

---

### 3. Robots.txt (Crawler Guidance)
- **File:** `app/robots.ts`
- **URL:** https://retirefree.app/robots.txt (auto-generated by Next.js)
- **Rules:**
  - Allow: All public pages
  - Disallow: /dashboard/, /api/, /auth/
  - Sitemap: Points to sitemap.xml

**What this does:**
- Prevents indexing of private pages
- Prevents indexing of API routes
- Tells crawlers where to find sitemap
- Saves crawl budget

---

### 4. Canonical URLs
- **Added to:** Privacy Policy, Terms of Service
- **Format:** `alternates: { canonical: 'https://retirefree.app/page' }`

**What this does:**
- Prevents duplicate content issues
- Tells Google which URL is the "official" version
- Important for SEO ranking

---

### 5. FAQ Schema (Rich Snippets)
- **Added to:** Homepage (app/page.tsx)
- **Type:** FAQPage schema with 5 questions
- **Questions:**
  1. Is my data secure?
  2. How is this different from a financial advisor?
  3. What if my situation changes?
  4. Can I cancel anytime?
  5. How does the AI work?

**What this does:**
- Can appear as rich snippets in Google search
- Increases visibility in search results
- Improves click-through rate
- Helps with voice search

**Example of what it looks like in Google:**
```
RetireFree - Retirement Calculator
https://retirefree.app
Calculate your optimal retirement withdrawal strategy...

‚ñº People also ask
  Is my data secure?
```
