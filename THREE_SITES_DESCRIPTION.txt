## SHORT VERSION (200 words)

We operate a comprehensive retirement planning ecosystem serving baby boomers (ages 55-75) across three integrated platforms:

**RetireFree.app** - AI-powered retirement withdrawal calculator helping retirees determine safe monthly withdrawal amounts from their savings. Uses Monte Carlo simulation with current market data to provide personalized recommendations. $15/month vs $5,000/year financial advisors.

**Where55.com** - National directory of 55+ active adult communities, retirement villages, and age-restricted neighborhoods. Helps baby boomers find ideal retirement locations with amenities, pricing, and community reviews.

**WhereAssistedLiving.com** - Comprehensive directory of assisted living facilities, memory care centers, and senior housing options. Assists families in finding quality care for aging parents or planning their own future care needs.

Together, these platforms serve the complete retirement journey: financial planning (RetireFree), lifestyle housing (Where55), and care planning (WhereAssistedLiving). Our audience actively seeks retirement planning books, financial guides, estate planning resources, and senior lifestyle products.

**Combined Traffic:** Targeting 50,000+ monthly visitors within 6 months across all three platforms.
