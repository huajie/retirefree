## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### "My application was rejected"
**Common reasons**:
- Low traffic (< 1000/month)
- Incomplete profile
- Poor quality content

**Solutions**:
- Wait 30 days and reapply
- Show proof of traffic (Google Analytics screenshot)
- Improve content quality on your site

### "I was approved but can't find my link"
**Solution**:
- Login to affiliate dashboard
- Look for "Get Links" or "Creative Assets"
- Copy your unique affiliate URL

### "No conversions after 2 weeks"
**Solutions**:
- Check link tracking (are people clicking?)
- Improve CTA (call-to-action)
- Create more content around the tools
- Email your users directly
