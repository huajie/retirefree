## ðŸŽ¯ Affiliate Marketing Strategy (HIGHEST ROI)

### Why Affiliates Work Best for RetireFree

1. **High Commission**: $50-$500 per referral (vs $0.50-$5 CPM for ads)
2. **Relevant**: Financial tools are EXACTLY what your users need
3. **Non-Intrusive**: Feels like helpful recommendations, not ads
4. **Trusted**: You're helping users find tools they actually need

### Top Affiliate Programs to Join

#### Investment Platforms
1. **Wealthfront** - https://www.wealthfront.com/affiliates
   - Commission: $50-$100 per funded account
   - Cookie: 30-90 days
   - Best for: Automated investing users

2. **M1 Finance** - https://m1.finance/affiliate
   - Commission: $50-$100 per funded account
   - Cookie: 30 days
   - Best for: DIY investors

3. **Betterment** - https://www.betterment.com/partners
   - Commission: $50-$150 per funded account
   - Cookie: 90 days
   - Best for: Beginner investors

#### Financial Planning Tools
4. **Personal Capital** - https://www.personalcapital.com/affiliate
   - Commission: $100+ per signup
   - Cookie: 90 days
   - Best for: Portfolio tracking

5. **YNAB (You Need A Budget)** - https://www.youneedabudget.com/referral-program/
   - Commission: $6 per signup
   - Cookie: 30 days
   - Best for: Budgeting

#### Brokerage Platforms
6. **Robinhood** - https://robinhood.com/us/en/about/affiliate/
   - Commission: $50-$200 per funded account
   - Cookie: 30 days
   - Best for: Stock trading

7. **Fidelity** - https://www.fidelity.com/affiliate/overview
   - Commission: Varies ($50-$150)
   - Cookie: 30 days
   - Best for: All-in-one retirement accounts

#### Credit Cards & Banking
8. **Credit Card Affiliates** (via networks)
   - Commission: $50-$200 per approval
   - Networks: CJ Affiliate, Rakuten, Impact
   - Best for: Balance transfer, rewards cards

9. **High-Yield Savings Accounts**
   - Marcus by Goldman Sachs: $50-$100 per funded account
   - Ally Bank: $50-$100 per funded account
   - Cookie: 30-90 days

## ðŸ“ˆ Tracking & Analytics

### Metrics to Monitor
1. **Click-through rate (CTR)** - % of users clicking affiliate links
2. **Conversion rate** - % of clicks that result in signups
3. **Revenue per visitor** - Total affiliate revenue / visitors
4. **Top-performing partners** - Which tools drive most revenue
5. **Content performance** - Which blog posts convert best

### Tools to Use
- **Affiliate Network Dashboards** - Track conversions
- **Google Analytics** - Track link clicks (UTM parameters)
- **Custom Dashboard** - Build simple tracking in Supabase
