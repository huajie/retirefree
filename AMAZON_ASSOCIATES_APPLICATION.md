## Website/App Description

**RetireFree.app** is an AI-powered retirement withdrawal calculator that helps retirees and pre-retirees (age 55+) determine how much they can safely withdraw from their retirement savings each month without running out of money.

Our platform uses advanced Monte Carlo simulation with 10,000 market scenarios and current 2026 market data (not the outdated 4% rule from 1994) to provide personalized withdrawal recommendations.

**Target Audience:**
- Retirees aged 55-75
- Early retirees (FIRE community)
- Pre-retirees planning their retirement
- DIY investors managing their own portfolios
- People seeking affordable alternatives to $5,000/year financial advisors

**Core Features:**
- Free retirement withdrawal calculator (no signup required)
- 401k withdrawal strategy optimizer
- Monte Carlo simulation with real market data
- Premium service: $15/month for ongoing monthly updates
- Educational blog content on retirement planning

**Monthly Traffic:** Growing (new site launched March 2026)
**Expected Monthly Visitors:** 15,000+ within 6 months
