## How to Apply on CJ

### Step 1: Login and Search
1. Go to https://members.cj.com/member/publisher
2. Login with your account
3. Click **"Advertisers"** → **"Advertiser Search"**
4. Search for advertiser name (e.g., "Personal Capital")

### Step 2: Review Requirements
Check for each advertiser:
- ✅ **Traffic Requirements:** Most want 1,000+ monthly visitors
- ✅ **Content Requirements:** Finance-related content (you have this!)
- ✅ **Compliance:** No spam, ethical marketing
- ✅ **Geography:** US traffic (specify this)

### Step 3: Craft Your Application

**Template for Application Message:**
```
Website: RetireFree.app
Niche: Retirement withdrawal planning and financial independence

Description:
RetireFree.app provides AI-powered retirement withdrawal recommendations
to help retirees determine safe spending levels. Our primary audience is:
- Age: 55-75 years old
- Income: $50K+ annual
- Assets: $500K+ in retirement accounts
- Behavior: Actively planning retirement finances

We would promote [ADVERTISER NAME] in:
- Recommended tools section on calculator results pages
- Blog articles about [relevant topic]
- Email newsletter to subscribers (launching Q2 2026)

Our content is educational, FTC-compliant, and focused on helping
retirees make informed financial decisions. We expect 500+ daily
visitors by end of March 2026, growing to 2,000+ by Q2.

Traffic sources:
- Organic search (Google)
- Direct (return visitors)
- Referrals from sister sites (where55.com, whereassistedliving.com)

Promotional methods:
- Text links in blog content
- Recommended tools sections
- Calculator result pages
- Comparison tables
```

### Step 4: Submit Applications

**Best Practice:**
- Apply to 5-10 advertisers per day (don't spam)
- Customize each application message
- Be honest about current traffic (even if low)
- Emphasize **target audience quality** over quantity
- Mention **sister sites** for credibility

### Step 5: Follow Up
- Check application status in 3-5 business days
- If rejected, ask why and reapply in 30 days
- If approved, deploy links within 7 days
