## 7. Pre-Written Responses to Common Questions

### "How is this different from [competitor]?"
"Great question! Unlike comprehensive planning tools (NewRetirement, Boldin), RetireFree focuses on ONE job: telling you how much to withdraw each month. We're simpler, more affordable ($15 vs. $120+/year), and our AI adapts to changing conditions automatically. Think of us as the specialized tool you check monthly, not the overwhelming spreadsheet you set up once and never update."

### "Can I trust AI with my retirement?"
"I totally understand the skepticism. Here's how we handle it: (1) Our AI uses conservative, time-tested withdrawal strategies as a foundation, then adapts based on your situation. (2) We show our reasoning so you can see WHY we recommend a certain amount. (3) You're always in controlâ€”treat our recommendations as guidance, not gospel. (4) We're transparent about our methodology and never promise unrealistic returns."

### "Why not just use the 4% rule?"
"The 4% rule was great when it was created (1994), but it has major flaws: (1) It doesn't adapt to market conditions, (2) It ignores your personal situation, (3) Research shows it's too conservative for some retirees and too aggressive for others. RetireFree uses a dynamic approach that adjusts monthly based on actual conditionsâ€”helping you spend more when it's safe and pull back when needed."

### "What if markets crash?"
"This is exactly what RetireFree is designed for. When markets drop, our AI recalculates your safe withdrawal amount and gives you a clear recommendation. You'll know immediately if you need to adjust spending temporarily. This beats the alternative: panicking and making emotional decisions, or blindly following the 4% rule and potentially running out of money."

### "Is my data secure?"
"Yes. We use bank-level encryption (256-bit) for all data. Your information is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data to third parties. You can disconnect your accounts anytime. We're GDPR compliant and follow industry best practices. For MVP, we actually don't even connect to your real accountsâ€”you input values manually, giving you full control."

"Not at all. If you can use email, you can use RetireFree. The calculator is 5 simple questions. The dashboard shows one main number: 'Safe to withdraw $X this month.' No spreadsheets, no jargon, no complex charts (unless you want to see them)."

### "Can I cancel anytime?"
"Absolutely. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel with one click via your account settings. You'll keep access through the end of your billing period. We'd rather have happy customers who stay because they want to, not because they're locked in."

### "What's your business model long-term?"
"Subscriptions ($15/month) are our core revenue. Eventually we'll add: (1) Premium tier ($49/month) with CFP consultations and advanced tax planning, (2) Financial advisor partnerships (white-label our tech), (3) Educational content/courses.
